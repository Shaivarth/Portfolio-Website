Health-Hack 2025

Competed in a Health Hackathon jointly organized by VIT Bhopal University, India and John Hopkins University, USA on 18–19 February 2025!

We successfully cleared 2 rounds of the hackathon. While we didn’t make it to the final round, the experience was incredible, and we’re proud of what we built.

It was an interesting experience tackling real-world healthcare challenges while applying our skills in Cyber Security, Web Development and UI UX.
